About: The Game




What's the purpose?

 The Game  is designed to be a fun, yet difficult puzzle that allows you to showcase your problem solving skills in an unfamiliar environment. The main purpose of this challenge is to get you to try and think outside the box. The only 'rule' is to win. How you do it, is up to you. Everything you see is there for a reason.

There may be more than meets the eye.
