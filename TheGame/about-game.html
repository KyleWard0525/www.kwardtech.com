About: The Game


What is The Game?

The Game is an online candidate evaluation tool developed for GlobalTech by Katari-Wolf Technology. This tool is used by GlobalTech to evaluate potential candidates for technical positions that often require a certain level of critical thinking and reasoning skills.

Here's how it works:  
-   There are 5 buttons. Each button can be ON (green) or OFF (red).
-   The buttons must be enabled in a specific order. Some buttons may be connected to each other.
-   If enabled in the wrong order, the connected button(s) may be effected.
-   You will have exactly 124 seconds to find the correct sequence and enable the buttons.
-   If you succeed, you will move on to the next phase.
-   If you fail, you will no longer be considered for a position at GlobalTech.
